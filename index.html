Women's Football 2017
#weplaystrong

Follow Women's European Football Championship 2017
Women’s Football 2017 is an iOS-only app designed specifically for the upcoming tournament, aiming to raise interest in women's football.

Support an upcoming phenomena
Women’s football is gradually gaining popularity all over the world. However, we see that there’s still a long way to go, and women’s football deserves much more attention. Join the ongoing process by following the most important tournament of the year.

Personalized Notifications
Subscribe to your favorite teams or matches to receive gentle notifications about them. If those notifications disturb you, you can easily unsubscribe from them right from the notification screen.

State-of-the-art Apple Watch complication
Women’s Football 2017 fully leverages the power of Apple Watch complications, giving you the simplest way to follow the flow of the tournament right from your wrist. Simply look at your watch face to see the score of the current match, or the time of the upcoming one. Rotate the digital crown to review the previous events or see what’s next. All watch faces are supported.

100% free and Open Source
Women’s Football 2017 is a completely non-commercial project built completely on enthusiasm and huge love for football.
